About me

Hello friend, I am Chipik. I am a playful and fluffy doggo with awesome and big ears that hear everything that happens around. Whenever I hear some bizarre sound, of course, I warn my human. However, my human usually gets upset and angry as I am small, but my voice is loud. I am actually proud of my voice. Dogs on the street usually afraid of me as I pass by.

Actually, I am a cute boy, I really love to go for a walk. Whenever you will ask me if I want to go for a walk, I will immediately start barking in a high voice, running around and jumping on highest objects.

By the way, I have a lot of interesting toys. But they are only mine! These are a small brown mouse, yellow juice bottle cap, small red rubber dog, which makes weird loud sounds every time I bite it, there is also a blue Angry Bird.
